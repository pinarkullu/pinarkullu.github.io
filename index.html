Current Projects

  • MMASD Dataset Enhancement
    Enhancing the MMASD (Multimodal Dataset for Autism Intervention Analysis) dataset for improved precision, by tracking and cropping individuals from videos and extracting multimodal data per person. Additionally training machine learning models for human activity recognition to facilitate a comprehensive case study.
  • Revolutionizing Midwifery Education - VR-Based Practical Training Platform
    Developing a VR platform with an interactive avatar representing a pregnant individual to assist midwifery students in practicing responses to health-related questions.
  • AI/ML-Enabled Surrogate Modeling for Coastal Environments
    Developing AI/ML-enabled surrogate modeling techniques to address multi-scale challenges resulting from climate change impacts on coastal environments.
  • Generative Models of Realistic Brain Mechanical Property Maps
    Generating subject-specific mechanical property maps of the brain using MRI data to advance our understanding of brain mechanics.
